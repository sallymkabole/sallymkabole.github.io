Research

Multilingual Health QA System

CMU Africa · 2024 – 2025

A knowledge graph-backed question answering system for chronic disease health information in Kinyarwanda and English. Built for low-resource language contexts in East Africa, the pipeline anchors every response to structured facts retrieved from a biomedical knowledge graph — reducing hallucination while supporting multilingual access.

Key design decisions

  • All reasoning kept in English; translation at input and output only, to avoid compounding errors
  • Factual content blocks from SPARQL queries — the LLM handles fluency, not factual recall
  • OWL ontology with East African food vocabulary (LocalFood subclass) not in standard biomedical ontologies
  • Evaluated on Completeness, Fluency, and Relevance by independent Kinyarwanda-speaking annotators
